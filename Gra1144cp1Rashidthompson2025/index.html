INGREDIENTS

Getting the recipe together

  • 2 chicken breast
  • Salt, Pepper, Garlic powder, smoked paprika
  • 3 Tbsp of butter
  • buffalo Wild wings Honey BBQ Sauce
  • 4 Buns
  • 8 pieces of cooked bacon
  • Pepperjack Cheese
  • lettuce
  • fresh Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • (Condiments of your choosing)

STEPS

  1. Bake the bacon on a sheet pan in the oven at four-hundred (400°F) degrees for twenty(20) to twenty-five(25) minutes.
  2. Slice the chicken breast in half lengthwise to make them thinner.
  3. Then season your chicken breast on both sides with salt, pepper, garlic powder, smoked paprika
  4. Once seasoned, heat a skillet over medium high-heat and melt 2tbsp of butter
  5. Then add the chicken breast to the skillet and let it sear in the skillet for a couple of minutes
  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and brush on some of the BBQ sauce to the tops of the chicken
  7. Next, put the chciken breast in the oven and have the oven set four-hundred (400°F) degrees for ten(10) to twelve(12) minutes to fully cooked
  8. Melt another piece of butter (1 tbsp) in the skillet over medium-heat and toast the buns to golden brown.
  9. Once the chicken is cooked, add some cheddar cheese and put back in the oven for just a few minutes to just melt the cheese.
  10. Lastly, combined your creation into a sandwich, add the cooked bacon, lettuce, tomatoes and onions to your burger in anyway you see fit.
